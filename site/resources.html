Resource Center

Enjoy browsing our resources! The Center is designed to educate and inform professionals, the public, and our members about the harms of gas-powered leaf blowers and actions taken to end the use of this heavily polluting and hazardous tool. Legislation, voluntary action, and outreach & education are critical to accelerating the transition to quieter and healthier alternatives.

To submit a potential resource for consideration, email: info@quietcleanalliance.org.
