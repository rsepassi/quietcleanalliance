Member Organizations

Member organizations are the core of the Alliance. Each group below is working to educate the public and landscapers about the harms of gas leaf blowers, pass policies restricting or prohibiting them, encourage efforts to transition away from these harmful tools immediately, and promote the many viable alternatives. We share information and strategies, support each other's campaigns, and work with other groups to build awareness of the issue and grow the movement's political strength.
