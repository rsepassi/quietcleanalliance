Welcome to the Quiet Clean Alliance

We are a network of independent campaigns across the U.S. working to eliminate gas leaf blowers from our communities due to their considerable health and environmental harms. Our goal is to accelerate the shift away from gas leaf blowers at local, state, and national levels.

Here is a brief description of what we do. We invite you to visit our other pages to learn more about the Quiet Clean Alliance. Be sure to explore our Resource Center and Latest News.

Our Activities

Local

We work cooperatively to accelerate the shift off gas leaf blowers and other gas-powered land care tools in each of our municipalities and counties.

State

We track active bills in state legislatures that support the reduction or elimination of gas land care equipment. We act together to support and defend policy efforts in each of our states.

National

We advocate to restore the Federal Noise Control Office and for action by Congress and federal agencies to eliminate gas leaf blowers and other gas-powered land care equipment.
